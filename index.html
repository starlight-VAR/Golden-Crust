Our Story

Founded in 1985 by master baker Jacques Laurent, Golden Crust began as a small neighborhood bakery with a simple mission: to create exceptional bread using time-honored techniques.

Today, under the leadership of Jacques' daughter Marie, we continue this tradition while expanding our offerings to include a full range of artisan breads, pastries, and custom cakes.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, including flour from regional mills, dairy from family farms, and seasonal fruits from nearby orchards.
